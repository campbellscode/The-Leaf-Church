A Word From The Pastor

We are Apostolic in doctrine and Pentecostal in experience. We are here to offer biblically sound healing, beginning with an Acts 2:38–39 experience: repentance, baptism in the name of Jesus Christ, and the infilling of the Holy Ghost with the evidence of speaking in other tongues as the Spirit gives utterance. We believe in one God, and His name is Jesus Christ. We also believe in a lifestyle of holiness and separation from the world unto the Lord.
