Kroger Community Rewards

About The Program

Turn everyday shopping into consistent support for our organization. When you link your Kroger Plus Card, a percentage of eligible purchases is donated at no extra cost to you!

Easy Enrollment
1. Create a digital account if you don't already have one.
2. Link your Card to our organization.
3. Our organization earns.

Please see the other tabs for exact instructions on how to enroll via the mobile app or the Kroger website.
No Extra Cost
After linking your card, any transactions moving forward using the Shopper’s Card number associated with your digital account will be applied to the program, at no added cost to you. Kroger donates annually to participating organizations based on your percentage of spending as it relates to the total spending associated with all participating Kroger Community Rewards organizations.
Support
If you have any questions, please contact Kroger's Customer Service Center .

How to Enroll

Please follow these steps to link your card to our organization via the Kroger app on Android:

  1. Go to the Kroger app and sign in or create an account.
  2. Open the menu and scroll down to Community Rewards.
  3. Search for our organization by name, The Leaf Church.
  4. Select our organization and click Enroll.
  5. Use your Kroger Plus Card or Alt ID at checkout—your purchases now support our church! Thank you!

If on a desktop/laptop computer, please follow these steps:
